What is the CAL-CP?

The CAL-CP is a linear decision tree to guide through the ICD-11 diagnostic criteria of chronic pain. It was developed for pain specialists, practice, and research. It is based on the guidelines of the Society for Medical Decision Making Committee on Standardization of Clinical Algorithms (1992). The aim of the CAL-CP is to facilitate and standardize the classification process; we cannot guarantee that diagnoses are correct.
